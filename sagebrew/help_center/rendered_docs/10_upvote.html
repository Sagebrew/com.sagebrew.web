What should I use Upvoting for?

Upvote questions and solutions that provide relevant content to the ongoing discussion. If you find an solution to be particularly helpful give it an Upvote and help raise awareness of that solution. If you think a particular question merits more traffic, give it an Upvote to help it on its way.

What happens when I Upvote?

When you Upvote a question or solution you have expressed interest in that question or solution. Upvoting doesn't harm you, and in fact, may help you acquire Badges!
