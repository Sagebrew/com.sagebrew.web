Ability to Comment

Reputation needed: 20

Description

You've gained the ability to Comment on other member's Questions and Solutions! Comments are intended for suggestions, criticisms, feedback, and clarification. Comments should be aimed at helping to improve a Solution or Question.

When to Comment

When you can assist another user with a Solution or Question. If you see a Solution that is close but you believe it's missing something or needs help being pushed in the right direction, you should leave a Comment that offers relevant material aimed at improving it. Comments are about helping derive the best Solutions possible and making Questions understandable and complete. Comments are there to help you have a conversation with another individual, on a specific piece of content, and help to iteratively improve upon it.

When not to Comment

You should refrain from Commenting if your Comment doesn’t add anything significant to the conversation. No “me too!” or “That’s stupid” types of Comments. Those take up space and time, and will inevitably be Flagged and removed. Use an Upvote or Downvote if you disagree or agree in such a way. You should also refrain from Commenting if your Comments are offensive, inappropriate, or spam.
