What happens when I donate to a Candidate?

After you've indicated you'd like to donate to a candidate you will receive an email stating that you will donate "x" amount of money to candidate "y" upon them reaching their Donation Goal. You will not be charged and your donation will not be provided to the candidate until their Donation Goal has been reached, at which time you will receive a second email with a notification that confirms your donation. The representative you donated to will also receive notice that you have donated to their cause. In the instance that a candidate does not reach their Donation Goal, you will not be charged.
