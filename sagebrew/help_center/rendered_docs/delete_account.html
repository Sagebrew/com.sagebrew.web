How do I delete my account?

We are very sad that you're considering the possibility of deleting your account and leaving the Sagebrew community. If there is something you're having difficulty with or a reason you're looking to delete your account please let us know by clicking the in the bottom right corner and we'll do everything we can to resolve the issue.

Overview

Please be aware that after you delete your account it will no longer be accessible and cannot be reinstated. This means any reputation you've gained will be lost.

Steps

  1. To delete your account first click Settings in the navigation bar.

  2. Next click on the "Delete Account" button in the left hand navigation of the settings.

  3. Click the "Delete Account" on the page.

  4. Confirm that you want to delete your account.
