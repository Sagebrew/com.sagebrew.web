How do I find topics I'm interested in?

We understand that each of our users will have different interests and agendas, therefore we've made it easy to follow topics that you're interested in. When creating an account we will ask you to select the topics that interest you. By checking the box next to any of the topics we present, you will tailor your feed to provide discussions associated with your selection.
