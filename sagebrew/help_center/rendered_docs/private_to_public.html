Can I convert a private conversation into a public one?

You cannot convert a private conversation to a public conversation. When you start a private conversation you adopt the promise that all proposed solutions and comments will remain private to protect the users involved. If you have a private conversation that you'd like to move to the public conversation area you will need to resubmit the question in the public forum and begin the process again.

The primary reason for this is to protect other users who have contributed to a private conversation but might not want their responses made public.
