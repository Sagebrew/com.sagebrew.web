What does it mean if a Conversation is "Protected"?

A Protected Conversation is a conversation that has been around for at least a day and a member from the Admin Council has deemed "Protected." What this means is that in order to further contribute to the conversation, a user must have at least 50 Reputation. A conversation may become Protected if:

  • It is generating excess Spam
  • There are no relevant new Solutions being posed
  • A Solution has been determined, but it has not yet received 1000+ Upvotes

If a Solution reaches 1000+ Upvotes it becomes Seasoned.
