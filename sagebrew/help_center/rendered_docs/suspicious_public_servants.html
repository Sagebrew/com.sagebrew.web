How do I report suspicious activity by a Representative?

If you have a valid reason for believing that a representative or candidate should be reported for suspicious activity, you may flag that representative by going to that representative's Quest and clicking the . This will open a ticket where you'll be able to offer a reason for flagging that representative.

Valid reasons for flagging a representative:

  • They have not given a proper update after reaching a Donation Goal
  • They have not spent their donation funding as they had promised
  • They have been inactive and/or unresponsive for an extended period of time

DO NOT flag a representative because you don't agree with their policies or beliefs. Flagging representatives is reserved for suspicious or illegal activity only, not personal bias.
