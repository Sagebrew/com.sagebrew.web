We want a transparent government where elected officials use facts and stats to create policy. Where our government uses technology to solve problems and where officials care more about solutions than their re-election. Getting there starts with advocacy. If you share our vision or have policy that fits the bill we'll do everything we can to help you enact change.

Fundraising

Unlike other crowdfunding platforms we specifically focus on advocacy and political campaigns. There's no time limits or delays on releasing donations. We know every dollar counts to your movement and we're here to help you succeed.

Volunteers

Money isn't the only thing you need to succeed. We know how important volunteers are to getting things done so we make it as easy for people to volunteer for your movement as it is to donate.

Enabling Transparency

We provide you with the means to update supporters on what you're doing as well as get their feedback. Using Updates and Conversations you can engage and grow your movement.

Community Connection

Movements can often times come down to your ground game and engagement with the community. We make sure your movement gets seen by every member it affects and each member that is passionate about the cause. We'll help you get your message out specifically to people that matter so you're not wasting time.

Online Presence

We want to empower you to share your movement with the world. You'll be able to setup a one pager with images, videos, and anything else you need to get the word out. We handle making it responsive to ensure you're reaching supporters on their phones, tablets, and any other device hitting the market.

Security

We take personal information and credit card security seriously. We're PCI level 1 compliant and will never sell your personal data behind your back.

Scalable

From a local rally to a world wide movement we've got you covered. Our platform is built to scale so whether you're expecting $50 in donations or over a $1,000,000 you don't need to worry about losing opportunities.

Customer Support

You need to focus on your movement, we know that. That's why we provide an integrated support platform that you and your donors can reach us with at anytime. We respond to every email and every message we get.

No Contract, No Surprises

We believe we should only make money if you make money. That's why signing up and hosting your movement is Free. Then we take 5% + 30¢ from each donation to keep the lights on. 2.9% + 30¢ goes to processing fees and 2.1% helps us to create new features for you and your donors. We'll direct deposit donations minus the fee into your bank account as soon as they are made.

Who Can Join?

Anyone can join! We focus on the tools you need and leave the agenda to you.
